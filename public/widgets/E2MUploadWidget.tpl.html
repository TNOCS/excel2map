Upload your data from Excel2map

Uploading data can be performed in multiple ways. Unless your network configuration prevents it, you can simply press the upload-button in the Excel-templates to upload data automatically. When that doesn't work, use one of the options below to create your map.

Option 1. Paste from clipboard.

Paste data from your clipboard to this textfield. Click the upload-button in the Excel-sheet first, to make sure the data has been copied to your clipboard.

Option 2. Upload file.

Upload the *.json file that you saved on your computer.

Option 3. Drag the file onto this widget.

Drag the json-file from your explorer-program to this box.
